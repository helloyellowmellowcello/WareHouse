Our Story

THE STORY

In 1934, Frances and Linda Ware started a furniture store in their hometown of Jackson, MI. The sisters continued the family tradition of building furniture, but their entrepreneurial spirit led them to start selling their creations. In the post-war housing boom, WareHouse® expanded across the mid-west to become a trusted name in home furnishings.

Today, WareHouse® has locations across Continental United States, providing contemporary designer furniture but still retaining that small, hometown feel. We are currently helmed by our Founders' capable successors and daughters, Frances Christiansen and Linda Wright.

Whether you are buying your first couch, re-modeling your kitchen, or moving to a new city, we're here to help you decorate the home of your dreams!
